Sapphire Cup

Congratulations to Yeti FC for achieving a 3rd place on the 2018 Sapphire Cup hosted by Machhapuchhre FC in Sydney, NSW. Excellent job by the guys not only on the tournament but the hard work leading up to the tournament. The team demonstrated excellent teamwork and commitment in the blazing 35+ degree heat overall 6 matches played in the two days. The young team proceeded comfortably through the 3 group stage games to qualify to the knockout stage as group winner. The team faced the tournament favorites, the host themselves, in the semi-final and the game tied 0-0 in normal time and extra-time. In the thrilling penalty shootout, the boys, unfortunately, missed their chance to play a final by loosing in sudden-death. The boys played an exciting third-place match against Sagarmatha FC and won the game 1-0 to end their adventurous Sapphire Cup 2018 campaign.

U-Nite Cup 2018

Yeti FC is partaking in the 2018 U-Nite Cup organized by the Melbourne Chinese Soccer Association with event partners Melbourne Victory and Football Victoria (FV). The tournament is played over the summer amongst 20 teams across Victoria representing 20 communities. The tournament starts from the 4th of November with matches played at Darebin Stadium.
