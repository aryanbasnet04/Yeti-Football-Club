Yeti FC Championship

Yeti Football Club has been organising an annual National Championship – ‘Kandel Consultancy Cup’, which is held March 9 – March 10 (Labour Day Weekend) since 2010. In 2020 a total of $10,000 was awarded in cash prize.

The tournament brings to the pitch a celebration of our cultural traditions and diversity, an enrichment event that links all Nepalese football teams from all over Australia. This Football Carnival is a gathering for our Nepalese youths to kick for a common purpose.

Thirty-two football teams from all over Australia, more than 500 players and well over 1000 football fans grace the field annually. Amongst these participants, there are also well-respected National Football players of Nepal, international referees and respected members of the footballing community in Nepal and Australia.

We would like to extend our sincere gratitude and appreciation for all of the support provided by our Sponsors, Supporters, Media Partners and Volunteers. We are fortunate to have Football Federation Victoria (FFV) and Melbourne City Football Club as event partners in the past.

Come and witness it yourselves ” Two days of non-stop football carnival.”
